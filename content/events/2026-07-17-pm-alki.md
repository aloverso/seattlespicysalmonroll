We're hanging out at Jack Block Park plaza with an evening full of skating and community. This isn't a scheduled event with a finish line - it's a place to gather, meet new people, and enjoy the waterfront. Skate over to Alki Beach for food, bring your own snacks and drinks, roll around with friends, or just kick back and enjoy the vibes. 

Come for an hour or stay all night, and most importantly, bring the energy!

You can skate back whenever you're ready or catch the West Seattle Water Taxi, which runs until 11:00 PM. If you're planning to take the ferry during or after the route, be sure to bring shoes along for boarding.
