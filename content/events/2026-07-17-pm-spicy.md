Once the full Alki Commute group crosses into West Seattle, the Demon Horns route breaks away for an extra helping of hills while the chill group continues along the trail. 

We'll climb Avalon before dropping down Genesee for a fast, steep descent, then tackle another climb that's perfect for carrying speed. After that we'll head up Admiral for one more long downhill, with a few intersections that we'll hopefully catch on green. We'll regroup with everyone at Alki Beach for the afternoon festivities.
