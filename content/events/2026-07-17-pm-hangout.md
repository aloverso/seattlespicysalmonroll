For anyone arriving early for our afternoon/evening skates! Here's some things you could do while hanging out at the waterfront:

- Check out some of the classic Seattle food vendors, such as Ivar's Fish and Chips or Molly Moon's Ice Cream
- Visit the elevated overlook connecting Pike Place Market to the waterfront
- Ride the Seattle Great Wheel for panoramic views (about 20 minutes)
- Skate flow up and down the new waterfront path!
