Ease into Sunday with a relaxed urban skate that shows just how fun city skating can be. 

We'll cruise through Seattle Center beneath the Space Needle, stop for burgers, fries, or a shake at the iconic Dick's, then roll through downtown and South Lake Union at a comfortable, social pace. 

This route is designed as a welcoming introduction to skating in the city, with plenty of opportunities to practice navigating streets, curbs, and intersections alongside experienced skaters. Whether you're looking to build confidence beyond the trails or simply enjoy a fun cruise through some of Seattle's most iconic neighborhoods, this is the perfect way to wrap up the weekend.

We're keeping this as chill as possible, but we rate this route 2/5 because there are some hills and traffic intersections. It's impossible to fully avoid hills in the Seattle urban center!
