Our Saturday night party skate is all about good music, good people, and a beautiful Seattle sunset.

We'll roll through Ballard at a mellow pace with a shared community playlist, so bring your portable speakers (and download Spotify) to help provide the soundtrack!

Add to the playlist: [⚙️ Steampunk 2 Playa 🏝️](https://open.spotify.com/playlist/2cO1nc7vsmjEMy28fB7vk9?si=9EsgCu_DTmqWNRJQqqRwXw&utm_source=copy-link&pi=LrT2jfVXSBWkR&pt=bfbe2f1456beeb8125b72d25c8052dbc)

Along the way we'll stop at Pagliacci Pizza and 7-Eleven for food and drinks before continuing to Golden Gardens to dance and watch the sun go down behind the Olympic Mountains.

Once the sun has set, we'll skate back to Gas Works Park together where we will get a beautiful view of Seattle’s night skyline across Lake Union!

**Be sure to bring lights** — we'll be skating after dark!
