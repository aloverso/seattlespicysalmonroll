Meet us at Pier 62 for a relaxed cruise along Seattle's beautiful new waterfront. A one-spice group will enjoy an easy pace along the waterfront path, while a two-spice group picks up the speed just a bit. 

We'll skate north past Olympic Sculpture Park and onto the Elliott Bay Trail before regrouping near the pedestrian bridge. From there we'll reverse course and roll back to Pier 62 together for a perfect summer evening skate.
